# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1][cov].

[cov]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

In short: be kind, be patient with people who are learning robotics
for the first time, assume good intent, and disagree without making
it personal.

## Reporting

If you see behavior that violates the above, contact the maintainer
listed in `SECURITY.md` (`security@teddybot.ai`). Reports are handled
confidentially.

The maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, commits, code,
issues, and to block contributors whose behavior is harmful to the
community.
